Technology Enablement Services Agreement

This Technology Enablement Services Agreement ("Agreement") outlines the terms and conditions under which we provide services to enable businesses to adopt, implement, and optimize technology solutions. By engaging our services, the Client ("you") agrees to the terms outlined herein.

Scope of Services

Our services include, but are not limited to:

  • Technology consulting and strategy development
  • Implementation of software and technology solutions
  • Customization, integration, and data migration
  • Training and support for technology solutions

Responsibilities

Our Responsibilities: We will provide the services in a professional and timely manner, adhering to industry standards and best practices. We will ensure that our solutions align with your business requirements as agreed upon during the engagement.

Your Responsibilities: You agree to provide timely access to required systems, data, and personnel, as well as to collaborate and provide feedback to ensure the success of the engagement.

Fees and Payment

The fees for the services will be outlined in a separate Statement of Work (SOW) or proposal. Payment terms, including due dates and accepted payment methods, will also be specified in the SOW.

Confidentiality

Both parties agree to protect the confidentiality of each other's proprietary and confidential information. This obligation extends beyond the termination of the Agreement.

Term and Termination

This Agreement will remain in effect until the completion of services unless terminated earlier by either party with a 30-day written notice. Termination will not affect the obligations to pay for services already rendered.

Limitation of Liability

Our liability for any claims arising from this Agreement is limited to the fees paid for the services that gave rise to the claim. We are not liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising under this Agreement will be resolved through mediation. If mediation fails, the disputes will be subject to arbitration in accordance with the rules of the jurisdiction agreed upon by both parties.

Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements, discussions, and understandings regarding the subject matter herein.
