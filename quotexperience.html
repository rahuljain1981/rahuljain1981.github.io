Quote Experience (QuoteX)

A smarter Salesforce estimation tool for rapid scope of work automation and instant SOW generation

QuoteX Admin Setup & Full Documentation - Videos

Quote Experience is a purpose-built Salesforce estimation tool that simplifies the way teams scope, estimate, and deliver proposals. From scope of work automation to serving as a reliable SOW generator, it accelerates every stage of Salesforce services quoting. Ideal for delivery teams, pre-sales consultants, and partners focused on professional services forecasting.

Key Features

Scope Library

Document, build, collaborate and iterate on project-based scope items with reusable scope templates.

Timeline Library

Build from scratch or use and modify existing timeline templates designed for common project types, durations, and sizes.

Resourcing Plan Templates

Resourcing Plan Templates automate role assignments. They use core resource hours to generate roles based on predefined rules, eliminating manual effort.

Staffing Rules Templates

Staffing Rules Templates establish staffing guidelines. They incorporate minimum role percentages, calculate average rates, and factor in all-inclusive rates based on predefined role assumptions.

Dynamic Document Templates

Create predefined formats for Statements of Work (SOWs). Build dynamic templates with various sections for diverse use cases, and instantly generate documents in PDF or gdoc format for easy moderation and approvals.

Rate Cards

Assign specific roles, rates and resourcing plan rules to scopes & timelines.

Pricing Engine

Fully define a quote by defining the contract type, applying discounts and adding in travel and expense costs & other costs.

Quick Service Quotes

Offers a Rough Order of Magnitude (ROM) calculation feature, providing preliminary cost estimates and resource allocations for early-stage project planning and budgeting.

Guided Selling

QuoteX simplifies the resourcing process and provides an intuitive feature for timeline building, allowing reviewers to prioritize quality over quantity.

Project Plans

Provides seamless creation of project plans upfront to share it with customer, giving the idea of your strategy for project success.

In-Flight Quote Rate Refresh

Easily update in-flight service quotes with the latest organization-wide rates and empowers Sales Engineers to quickly adjust for annual changes, ensuring accurate pricing without manual recalculations.
