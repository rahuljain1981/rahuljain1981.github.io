Home Carousel Banner

A versatile managed package designed to enhance the user experience by providing dynamic carousel banners for notifications across any Salesforce cloud.

Through simple configuration and metadata settings, you can control the content, order, and visibility of each carousel item, ensuring that users receive the information they need.

Key Highlights

Dynamic Carousel Creation

Easily create and manage carousel banners based on predefined configurations.
Customize the number of carousel items and their order to suit your needs.

Flexible Content Management

Activate or deactivate carousel items as required. Add titles, descriptions, and embed YouTube videos directly into the carousel items. Define the content body as HTML to create richer and more engaging carousel items.

User-Friendly Interface

Simple and intuitive interface for configuring and managing carousel items. Ensure that users receive the most relevant information in an easily accessible format.

Seamless Integration

Integrates seamlessly with any Salesforce cloud, leveraging existing data and workflows. Minimal setup required, allowing for quick deployment and immediate benefits.

Advanced Filtering Options

Filter carousel items based on the App Name Filter, ensuring users see only the most relevant information for their specific applications.
