Flexible Pricing: Volume-Based & Per User

Our pricing is designed to align with your organization's growth. We charge a per-user, per-month fee that decreases as your overall user count (volume) increases, ensuring cost-effectiveness as you scale.

  • ✅ Volume-Based Tiers: Lower per-user cost for larger teams.
  • ✅ Predictable Billing: Simple per-user subscription model.
  • ✅ Non-Profit Discount: Significant discounts available for registered non-profit organizations.
  • ✅ Support Included: Dedicated email support to assist with setup and troubleshooting.
