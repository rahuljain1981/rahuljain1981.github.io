Agentforce for Pre-Sales: Automate Your Pre-Sales Workflow

Pre-sales teams are often slowed down by manual tasks like scoping, pricing, and resource planning, which can lead to inaccuracies and longer sales cycles.
Agentforce for Pre-Sales, integrated with QuoteX, is an AI-powered solution that automates this entire process. Sales Engineers can use a simple, conversational interface to instantly generate quotes, build detailed scopes, create timelines, and produce a complete Statement of Work (SOW).
This streamlines the pre-sales workflow, ensuring pricing accuracy, improving efficiency, and empowering your team to close deals faster with data-driven confidence.
