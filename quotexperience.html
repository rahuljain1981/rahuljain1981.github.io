Got questions about QuoteX? You're not alone. Here are some answers to what others often ask.

QuoteX is an advanced Salesforce-native quoting tool designed for service organizations managing both products and services. It helps pre-sales and sales teams accurately scope projects, estimate costs, build timelines, and generate precise quotes directly within Salesforce.

QuoteX streamlines quoting by automating manual tasks and reducing errors through features like Scope Libraries, Resourcing Plan Templates, allowing sales engineers to save valuable time and respond to clients faster.

Scope Libraries provide reusable templates for capturing project scope and required effort. This feature ensures consistent documentation, enables quick scoping, and helps prevent underselling by accurately estimating resources and costs.

Yes, QuoteX allows you to calculate fees, costs, and discounts based on resources, hours, rates, and other factors, ensuring accurate pricing and consistency across quotes.

The SOW generator in QuoteX converts finalized quotes into professional Statements of Work (SOWs), including project scope, and resources. This streamlines client documentation and aligns expectations from the outset.

The BYOF capability allows organizations to add customized features to QuoteX, enabling it to meet unique business needs while maintaining full functionality within the Salesforce platform.

QuoteX integrates with Salesforce Chatter, allowing team members to collaborate in real-time on quotes. This feature promotes transparency and facilitates input from various stakeholders, enhancing accuracy and alignment.

While optimized for Salesforce Sales Cloud, QuoteX can also be used as a standalone application with platform licenses, providing flexibility for different organizational setups.

Currently, QuoteX is not optimized for mobile views. However, we have plans on our roadmap to develop a mobile-compatible version in the future to enhance accessibility on mobile devices.
